What is an Aurora Borealis?

The Aurora Borealis is a natural phenomenon that is caused by a solar storm from the sun.

Sometimes, the sun will let out a burst of gas that goes around the universe.

When it reaches Earth, it reacts with Earth's ozone layer and reacts with all the molecules in there,

this creates all the cool colors that you may see in the sky!

Do you know what colors are in this phenomenon?

There are:

  • greenish-yellow
  • blue
  • and red!
